---
title: "Weekly Sprint Report"
---

### Purpose

Summary of sprint progress, performance, and risks.

### Format

Subject:

```
Weekly Sprint Report - [Project Name] - Sprint [#]
```

Body:

```
1. Sprint Summary:

Sprint Start: [Date]
Sprint End: [Date]
Completed: X/Y tasks
Carry-over tasks: [List of tasks not completed]

2. Key Accomplishments:

✅ [Feature 1] - Delivered
✅ [Feature 2] - Tested & Approved

3. Challenges & Blockers:

[Describe blockers & solutions needed]

4. Next Sprint Focus:

[List of planned high-priority tasks]
```

### Delivery

Shared via email & discussed in the Sprint Review meeting.
